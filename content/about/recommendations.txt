##Reading##

* [Programming Pearls](http://www.cs.bell-labs.com/cm/cs/pearls/) by Jon Bentley
* [Effective Programming: More Than Writing Code](http://www.amazon.com/Effective-Programming-More-Than-Writing-ebook/dp/B008HUMTO0/ref=sr_1_1?s=digital-text&ie=UTF8&qid=1388341155&sr=1-1&keywords=Effective+Programming%3A+More+Than+Writing+Code) by Jeff "[Coding Horror](http://www.codinghorror.com/blog/)" Atwood
* [The Pragmatic Programmer: From Journeyman to Master](http://www.amazon.com/The-Pragmatic-Programmer-Journeyman-Master/dp/020161622X)
* [Seven Pillars of Wisdom](http://www.amazon.com/Seven-Pillars-Wisdom-Triumph-Complete/dp/1617201839/ref=sr_1_1?s=books&ie=UTF8&qid=1388341739&sr=1-1&) by T. E. Lawrence
* [Beyond Software Architecture](http://www.amazon.com/Beyond-Software-Architecture-Sustaining-Solutions/dp/0201775948/ref=sr_1_1?s=books&ie=UTF8&qid=1388341820&sr=1-1&) by Luke Hohmann
* [Refactoring: Improving the Design of Existing Code](http://www.amazon.com/Refactoring-Improving-Design-Existing-Code/dp/0201485672/) by Martin Fowler.
* [Web Operations](http://www.amazon.com/Web-Operations-Keeping-Data-Time/dp/1449377440/) by John Allspaw
