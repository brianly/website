I was asked to take <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/brianly/sets/72157629426130039/">a few photos</a> for this <a href="http://redsnakephilly.org/about/">event</a> by fellow pythonista <a href="https://twitter.com/#!/tpanzarella">Tom Panzarella</a>. I think they turned out OK for the most part - although the EF 70-200 L saved my arse in the end. It was difficult shooting with the lighting setup at the venue, but I'll be back there to get some more practice.
