Congratulations to <a href="http://blogs.msdn.com/dinoviehland/">Dino</a>, <a href="http://www.devhawk.net">Harry</a>, and the rest of the <a href="http://www.codeplex.com/IronPython">IronPython</a> team! They just released IronPython 2.0 which has parity with CPython 2.5. This release of IronPython includes release 0.9 of the <a href="http://www.codeplex.com/dlr">Dynamic Language Runtime (DLR)</a>.

Download it from <a href="http://www.codeplex.com/IronPython/Release/ProjectReleases.aspx?ReleaseId=8365">CodePlex</a>.
